Carpet Cleaning
Process of Carpet Shampooing

We use best quality professional grade imported DiverseyTR-101 and TR- 103 chemicals to shampoo Carpets / Rugs and Sofa for commercial units. Stain removal is done by specifically targeting the stains or spots, then a mixture of shampoo & detergent is applied directly on the fabric and scrubbed with the help of carpet brushes. Afterwards, the dirty foam and water is vacuumed with the help of a wet & dry vacuum cleaner machine. It leaves the sofa / carpet fabric cleaned and a little moist which dries within 4-5 hours time with normal air drying or a little bit of drying under the sun if needed.
